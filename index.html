About Me

I am a descendent of Mexican parents born and raised in NYC. I work at the Guggenheim Museum as the Director of Dining Operations. I am a graduate of the French Culinary Institute and have been working in the restaurant industry for over 18 years. Prior to entering the restaurant industry I was studying to become a computer technician. I started working in restaurants as a way to pay for my studies. When I did the transition from the Front of the House to the kitchen I quickly fell in love with cookinh. The rush as orders were coming in, the creativity and execution of a dish. The intricacies we place in each dish, composing and respecting the ingredients and using whats in season is an amazing feeling, and especially when a dish comes together. I am excited to return back to this journey of entering the tech industy, and start a new career as a web developer which will eventyally help me achieve my goal in a career with cloud computing.
